Welcome to DeFi’s newest daily and monthly jackpots

Lotto3 Jackpots are the only DeFi Community Rewards Program designed to give back directly to the community, and reward members for actively buying and selling project tokens and NFTs.

A quick tutorial on how to play:

First, Drawings are Transparent and Fair

All drawings are conducted using Verifiable Random Function (VRF) and distribution to winners is done using automation. All transactions can be verified on chain using Etherscan.

Daily DeFi Jackpot Eligibility

Community members must purchase and hold project tokens during the period between Daily DeFi Jackpot drawings.

One winner will be chosen from daily buys at random, no minimum purchase required.

To incentivize whale buys, the second winner will be chosen from the pool of buys 0.5 ETH or greater.

To Be Clear

Each buy is eligible for the Daily DeFi Jackpot drawing that immediately follows the purchase. If you buy two hours before the drawing and sell one hour after the drawing, you are only eligible for that Daily drawing. To be eligible for the drawing the next day, you must again purchase and hold until the next drawing.

Each buy constitutes an entry. If you make 4 buys of 0.1 ETH and 1 buy of 0.6 ETH, you will receive 5 entries into the random drawing and 1 entry into the whale drawing.

Monthly Mega Jackpots Eligibility

Community members who mint monthly Lotto3 NFTs will automatically be entered into Monthly Mega Jackpot drawings.

Lotto3 NFTs act as a community rewards card, and Monthly Mega Jackpot entries are the potential door prize. Beyond the monthly drawings, NFTs grant exclusive eligibility for second chance drawings, partner whitelist spots, and community governance voting.

Estimated values for Monthly Mega Jackpots, 500 – 1,000 ETH.

Second Chance Drawings

Second chance eligibility is limited to Lotto3 NFT holders. Minted Monthly Mega Jackpot NFTs are eligible for second chance drawings in perpetuity, meaning buying and holding an NFT in month one grants eligibility for as long as Second Chance Drawings continue.
